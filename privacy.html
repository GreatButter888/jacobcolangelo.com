Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23 April 2025

Jacob Colangelo (“I”, “me”, or “my”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how I collect, use, and store your personal information when you visit www.jacobcolangelo.com.

1. What I Collect

I may collect the following personal information:

  • Your email address when you subscribe to receive updates or content.

2. Why I Collect It

I collect your email address solely to:

  • Send you occasional updates, blog posts, or news related to my website and projects.
  • Better understand and communicate with people interested in my work.

3. How I Store and Protect Your Data

Subscriber information is initially collected via Formspree, a secure third-party form service. Once received, your data is transferred and securely stored on a self-hosted server managed by me.

The server is protected by security measures including:

  • Strong password and key-based authentication
  • Two-Factor Authentication (TFA)
  • Up-to-date software and firewall rules
  • Encrypted backups
  • Limited access controls (only I can access the data)

Your data is not shared with any third parties and is used exclusively for the purposes stated above.

4. Your Rights

In line with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information I hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletions
  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time via the unsubscribe link or by contacting me
